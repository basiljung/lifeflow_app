Program Overview

This document sets out the LifeFlow.Now™ 1:1 Coaching offer, including scope, outcomes, pricing, and legally binding terms. It is intended for clients with whom the program has been discussed.

The LifeFlow.Now Coaching System™. A structured 5-phase process combining Awareness × Self-Knowledge × Orientation × Action × Integration – designed for ambitious professionals who want a life that feels as good inside as it looks outside. Format: 1:1 coaching (online).

  • Session length: 55 minutes
  • Language: DE / EN / Swiss-German
  • Location: Online (Google Meet)

Coaching is not psychotherapy, medical, legal, tax, or financial advice. Client remains responsible for decisions and implementation.

Integration & Resilience
