Program Overview

This document sets out the LifeFlow.Now™ 1:1 Coaching offer, including scope, outcomes, pricing, and legally binding terms. It is intended for clients with whom the program has been discussed.

The LifeFlow.Now Coaching System™. A structured 5-phase process combining Awareness × Self-Knowledge × Orientation × Action × Integration – designed for ambitious professionals who want a life that feels as good inside as it looks outside. Format: 1:1 coaching (online).

  • Session length: 55 minutes
  • Language: DE / EN / Swiss-German
  • Location: Online (Google Meet)

Scope (high level)

  • Coaching, mentoring, and practical application support
  • Materials & exercises (shared digitally)
  • Optional between-session check-ins (see package)

Coaching is not psychotherapy, medical, legal, tax, or financial advice. Client remains responsible for decisions and implementation.

Who This Is For

  • Restless, stressed professionals seeking inner calm, focus, and energy.
  • People ready to apply insights weekly and be accountable.
  • Busy people who want personal growth without complexity.
  • Those ready to look deeper and make lasting change.
  • People who want to feel grounded and joyful even with high ambitions.

Objectives & Outcomes

Orientation
  • 1-Page Personal Operating Manual (values, strengths, motivators, limbic type, awareness level)
  • Decision Filters (simple yes/no rules & rationale)
  • Visionboard + Strategy (purpose & direction)
Execution
  • Execution Map (3 milestones, prioritized next actions)
  • Flow Protocol (triggers, blockers, re-entry steps)
  • LifeFlow.Now™ (AI) Coach — guidance aligned to your data
Integration & Resilience
  • Self-Awareness Habit Stack (daily/weekly practice)
  • Lasting confidence, inner peace, and resilience via pattern work
