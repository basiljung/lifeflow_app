Program Overview

This document outlines the LifeFlow.Now™ 1:1 Coaching offer, including scope, outcomes, pricing, and legally binding terms. It is intended for clients with whom the program has been discussed.

If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to contact me: basil.jung@lifeflow.now

The LifeFlow.Now Coaching System™ – A structured 5-phase process combining Awareness × Self-Knowledge × Direction × Action × Integration. The goal is not only to address your current challenges but also to deepen self-understanding and integrate new ways of living, creating lasting resilience and joy. The process is designed for ambitious professionals who want a life that feels as good inside as it looks outside.
Format: 1:1 coaching (online).

Coaching is not psychotherapy or medical, legal, tax, or financial advice. The client remains fully responsible for their own decisions and implementation.

Frame & Philosophy

I am not a big fan of contracts, but they clearly have their place and value in our world. Transparent communication is always essential. Whenever you feel misled, overlooked, or misunderstood, please reach out to me — even during a session (in that case, it won’t count as a coaching session).

For me, the co-creation aspect of coaching is key. It’s not about me working magic, nor about you carrying all the responsibility. It is about two conscious minds coming together to focus on your challenges and transformation — co-creating space for new awareness and solutions. This is not only the power of coaching — it is the power of genuine human connection.

Values worth highlighting here: Responsibility, dedication, mindfulness, hard work, trust, respect, transparency, openness, empathy, reliability, humor, progress, and professionalism.

Objectives & Outcomes

1# – Awareness
  • Self-Awareness Habit Stack (daily/weekly practices)
  • Integral thinking: uniting mind, heart, body, and soul in the individual, collective, subjective, and objective context.
2# – Self-Knowledge
  • 1-Page Personal Operating Manual (values, strengths, motivators, limbic type, awareness level)

3# – Direction · Purpose
  • Decision Filters (simple yes/no rules & rationale)
  • Vision Board + Strategy (purpose & direction)
4# – Action
  • Execution Map (milestones, prioritized next actions)
  • Understanding and integrating the cycle of creation
  • Flow Protocol (triggers, blockers, and re-entry steps into flow)
  • LifeFlow.Now™ (AI) Coach — accountability partner in your pocket
5# – Integration
  • Greater effectiveness and discipline through the right activities and the supportive motivation behind them.
  • Increased self-confidence and resilience through recognizing recurring patterns, shadow work, and conscious integration.
  • Lasting inner peace, deep trust, and joy through self-reflection, self-awareness, and emotional intelligence.
