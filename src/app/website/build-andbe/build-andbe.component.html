Monthly sessions for people who drive change – and want to stay grounded while doing it.

Build & Be is an open Community of Practice for those who want to combine personal growth with real-world impact. In our monthly gatherings at the Impact Hub, we blend execution power with inner calm and mental clarity. The exchange helps you stay centered in a fast-paced world.

🌿 Who it's for:

For doers, visionaries, change-makers & purpose-driven minds – who aim high while staying in flow and balance.

🌿 What to expect:

A 20-minute impulse on a core topic – followed by open, guided exchange around a current challenge, question or perspective. Learning and growing together.

Topics: Awareness, needs, identifying and leveraging strengths, value-based decisions, emotional intelligence, flow & focus, resilience, drive & rest, inner & outer order, energy management, productivity, efficiency, ease in execution, digital overload, task management, conscious tech use, the cycle of creation, Spiral Dynamics & integral thinking.
