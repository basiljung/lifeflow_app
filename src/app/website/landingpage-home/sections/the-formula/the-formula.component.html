    1# – Orientation · Resilience
    • Map your inner landscape: thoughts, emotions, behaviors—and the ways you block yourself.
    • Establish your standpoint using salutogenesis (what creates health and resilience).
    • Practice presence. Use an integral view—Head (clarity), Heart (connection), Hands (doing).
    2# – Self-Understanding · Self-Awareness
    • Look at biology and neurobalance: how body states shape mood, focus, and energy.
    • Clarify values, strengths, needs, and motivators.
    3# – Align · Strategy
    • Define authentic goals and purpose.
    • Translate them into structure, priorities, habits, and simple systems.
    • Design for motivation and joy, not just willpower.
    4# – Live · Cultivate Flow
    • Act, iterate, and keep going.
    • Align thinking, feeling, and action; integrate emotions with awareness and inner strength.
    • Review, correct, and create. Add play and celebration so learning sticks.
