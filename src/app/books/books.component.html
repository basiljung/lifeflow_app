Flow is a psychological state of deep concentration and full engagement in a single activity or in life itself. Your sense of time shifts, and awareness becomes fully anchored in the present moment. Flow is the state in which we create and experience genuine joy, fulfillment, and aliveness.

For a flow state to emerge, several conditions need to be in place:

  1. Clearly defined goal
  2. Balance between skills and challenge
  3. Concentration and focus: Flow occurs when a person is fully immersed in the present activity and filters out distractions.
  4. Joy and meaning
  5. Immediate feedback: “Am I moving in the right direction?” (e.g., when the code runs)
